Article 2: The "Doctrine of Skill" Jurisprudence

SR Ludo is a specialized platform hosting Games of Skill. Under the Constitution of India and various landmark precedents (e.g., K.R. Lakshmanan v. State of Tamil Nadu), games where success depends predominantly on the player's cognitive skills are not gambling.

Ludo on our platform requires players to utilize superior memory, probability calculation, strategic blocking, and time-management. Therefore, the fee paid to join a battle is a "Participation Fee" for a competitive skill event, not a wager. We explicitly state that this is NOT a betting or gambling platform.
