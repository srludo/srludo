Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At SR LUDO, we collect your phone number via secure authentication for account creation and login purposes. We also store your game transaction history and referral records in our secure Firebase database.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud.
  • To process withdrawals and credit winning balances.
  • To manage the referral program and commissions.

3. Data Security

We use Firebase Authentication and secure database rules to ensure that your private data is only accessible to you and authorized administrators. We have enforced HTTPS (SSL) encryption across our entire domain to protect data transmission.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (a Google product) for backend services and Phone.email for secure OTP verification. These services have their own privacy policies for data handling.
