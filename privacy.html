2. LEGAL JURISPRUDENCE: THE DOCTRINE OF SKILL

SR Ludo operates as a pure-play Skill-Based Gaming Platform. We explicitly renounce any association with gambling, betting, wagering, or games of chance. Our platform is engineered for intellectual competition. Under Indian Constitutional law, specifically the landmark precedents set by the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India, any game where success depends predominantly on the user’s "Superior Knowledge, Training, Attention, Experience, and Adroitness" is classified as a Game of Skill.

Ludo on our platform involves complex decision-making, move optimization, mathematical risk assessment, and opponent psychology. Therefore, participation in our paid battles is a constitutionally protected professional activity, not an act of gambling.

3. AUTHENTICATION ARCHITECTURE: FIREBASE SECURE PROTOCOL

To eliminate the systemic risks associated with traditional SMS-based OTPs—such as SIM swapping, network sniffing, and phishing—SR Ludo has transitioned to the Firebase Secure Authentication Infrastructure provided by Google Cloud.

3.1. Email-Identity Transposition

We collect your mobile number and, through a proprietary hashing algorithm, transpose it into a secure, unique Email-ID format within the Firebase Auth environment. This ensures that your actual credentials are never stored in plain text, making your account nearly immune to dictionary attacks or credential stuffing.

3.2. Real-Time Session Management

Every login session is managed via encrypted JSON Web Tokens (JWT). This means your session data is local to your device and our secure Firebase instance, preventing "Man-in-the-Middle" (MITM) attacks during the match-making process.

4. DATA FLOW: REALTIME DATABASE & SYNC GOVERNANCE

Our platform’s heartbeat is the Firebase Realtime Database. Unlike traditional SQL databases that suffer from latency, our NoSQL architecture allows for sub-second data synchronization.

Every move you make, every room code generated, and every battle status update is transmitted as an encrypted data packet. We store this "Match Metadata" to resolve disputes fairly. This data is isolated; no player can access another player's private data nodes, ensuring 100% database-level privacy.

5. IDENTITY VERIFICATION PROTOCOL (KYC)

To comply with anti-money laundering (AML) laws and to ensure a safe community, we require users to complete a Biometric-Linked Identity Verification.

5.1. Aadhaar Data Collection

We require the user’s Aadhaar number and a high-resolution photograph of the front side of the Aadhaar card. This data is used to:

  • Verify that the user has attained the legal age of 18 years.
  • Confirm that the user does not reside in the aforementioned "Restricted States."
  • Prevent the creation of duplicate/sybil accounts that disrupt game balance.

5.2. Financial Payout Verification

To deliver your winnings, we collect Bank Account Numbers, IFSC codes, and UPI IDs. This information is stored in a "Write-Only" database state, meaning once saved, it is locked and can only be modified via WhatsApp support after multi-factor verification.

6. IMAGE STORAGE: IMGBB ENTERPRISE API

All KYC documents and match-result screenshots (required for "I WIN" claims) are handled by the ImgBB Secure API.

Images are uploaded directly from the client-side to encrypted ImgBB buckets. This ensures that sensitive images are not stored on our primary web server, adding an extra layer of "Data Decoupling." Each image link generated is private and used only by our Admin panel for match settlement purposes.

7. THE DUAL-WALLET LEDGER SYSTEM

SR Ludo’s financial integrity is built on a segregated wallet system. We do not mix "Operational Funds" with "User Funds."

7.1. Cash Wallet (Deposits)

This wallet holds funds for game participation. These are classified as "Pre-paid Service Credits." These funds are protected and can only be used to join skill battles.

7.2. Winning Wallet (Realized Gains)

Only the amount won through skill matches is credited here. We employ a "Verification-First" withdrawal policy, ensuring that every rupee withdrawn is audited for fair play.
